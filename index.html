About Me

Logan is a Journalism + Design student studying at The New School in NYC. Currently, she is a writer and the social media director for The New School's chapter of Her Campus, the #1 online magazine for college women. She is also the editorial intern at InsideHook, the essential lifestyle guide for adventurous and established men. She enjoys writing about fashion, politics, women's health, and cultural trends.
